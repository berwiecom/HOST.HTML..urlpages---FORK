A little bit about JSPen

JSPen creates permanent web pages

That you can share anywhere.


It doesn't aim to be much, but you might find a couple of features interesting:

  1. Your entire pen is stored in the URL you share. Nothing is hosted anywhere.
  2. We pull front page posts from Twitter (tweet to @HTMLPins), and use url shortners for share links.
  3. There's no backend at all.

That means your pens can't be taken down, cost nothing, and once shared, anyone who obtains the URL can modify it (forever).

Your pens saved to "My Pens" are saved locally on your device, not the web. You're the only one who can see them, until you share them.

If this website is taken down, another can take it's place. The code is open-source (GitHub).


Based on a project by @jstrieb, called URL Pages (Source code)

We curate homepage posts by retweeting posts shared on Twitter with the tag #jspen.
